Keen Senses

Winged Ross the Stupendous has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight, hearing, or smell.

Variant: Familiar

Winged Ross the Stupendous can serve another creature as a familiar, forming a magic, telepathic bond with that willing companion. While the two are bonded, the companion can sense what Winged Ross the Stupendous senses as long as they are within 1 mile of each other. While Winged Ross the Stupendous is within 10 feet of its companion, the companion shares Winged Ross the Stupendous's Magic Resistance trait. At any time and for any reason, Winged Ross the Stupendous can end its service as a familiar, ending the telepathic bond.

Bite

Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.

Shortsword

Sting

Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 1 hour. If the saving throw fails by 5 or more, the target falls unconscious for the same duration, or until it takes damage or another creature uses an action to shake it awake.
